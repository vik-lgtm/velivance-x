Secure AI Adoption & Governance

We help organizations adopt AI safely, responsibly and at scale. Our approach covers security, access control, data governance, human-approval workflows, responsible-AI practices and enterprise-ready deployment standards — so you use AI confidently without losing control of sensitive data, systems or decisions. Our own compliance program targets ISO 42001 (AI management), ISO 27001, SOC 2 and GDPR.
